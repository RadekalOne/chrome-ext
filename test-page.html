How to Test the "Capture from Page" Feature

Step 1: Make sure the Pokemon Card Price Checker extension is installed and enabled.
Step 2: Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar.
Step 3: Click the "Capture from Page" button.
Step 4: The page should dim with a dark overlay. Click on any Pokemon card image below.
Step 5: The extension will analyze the card and show you pricing information.

Troubleshooting

  • If clicking "Capture from Page" shows an error, try refreshing this page and try again.
  • Make sure the extension has permission to access this page.
  • Check the browser console (F12) for any error messages.
  • If it still doesn't work, you can always upload a Pokemon card image directly from your computer.
